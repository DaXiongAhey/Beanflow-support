â“ FAQ

1) Does BeanFlow require Beancount files?

No. BeanFlow is a standalone double-entry accounting app. Beancount files are only used for optional import/export.

2) Which platforms are supported?

  • iOS (iPhone)
  • iPadOS
  • macOS
  • watchOS (quick summary)

3) Why does Apple Watch mainly show summaries?

Watch is designed for quick glance numbers: income, expenses, investment P/L, liabilities. Full editing and analytics are best on iPhone/iPad/Mac.

4) Is my data secure?

Your ledger stays on device and/or in your private iCloud database (if enabled). BeanFlow does not collect, upload, or analyze your financial data.

5) CSV / Beancount export?

Yes. CSV works with Excel/Numbers/Sheets. Beancount export supports single-file, and multi-file exports.
