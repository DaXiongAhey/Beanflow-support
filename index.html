‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Does BeanFlow require Beancount files?

No. BeanFlow is a standalone double-entry accounting app. Beancount files are only used for optional import and export.

Which platforms are supported?

  • iPhone (iOS)
  • iPad (iPadOS)
  • Mac (macOS)
  • Apple Watch (watchOS)

Why does Apple Watch show summaries only?

Apple Watch focuses on quick insights such as income, expenses, investments, and liabilities. Full editing and analysis are available on iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

Is my data secure?

All data is stored locally on your device or in your private iCloud database. BeanFlow never accesses or uploads your financial data.

Does BeanFlow support CSV / Beancount export?

Yes. CSV and Beancount export are supported. Some advanced export features may require a one-time unlock.
