Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-03-XX

1. Information Collection

BeanFlow does not collect any personally identifiable information. We do not track users, display ads, or perform analytics.

2. Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device or in your private iCloud database if you enable sync. Only you can access this data.

3. Files

Imported or exported Beancount / CSV files are processed entirely on device. They are never uploaded to any server.

4. Third-Party Services

BeanFlow uses only Apple system services such as iCloud (optional) and StoreKit for purchases.

5. Security

We rely on Apple platform security, including sandboxing, encryption, and biometric authentication.

6. Children’s Privacy

BeanFlow is not designed for children under 13 and does not collect children’s data.
